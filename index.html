IRS Enrolled Agent

Enrolled Agents (EAs) are the only federally-licensed tax practitioners who may represent taxpayers before the IRS when it comes to collections, audits and appeals. Under the Department of Treasury's Circular 230 regulations, EAs are granted unlimited practice rights to represent taxpayers before IRS and are authorized to advise, represent, and prepare tax returns.
